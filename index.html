Signature Case Studies

Context: Traditional paid media limited reach for sensitive service lines such as Cancer and Bariatrics.

Strategic Initiative: Introduced Reddit and contextual display campaigns to align messaging with research-phase audience behavior instead of relying solely on standard search and social channels.

Channel Strategy:

  • Reddit: Placed content-driven messaging within relevant health-focused communities aligned with discussion-based research behavior.
  • Contextual Display: Positioned ads alongside medically relevant content rather than broad demographic targeting.

Behavioral Insight: Users were in education and evaluation mode rather than immediate conversion mindset.

Optimization Pivot: Shifted from conversion-first to engagement-first model with refined landing experience and behavioral tracking alignment.

Results: Time on site improved from 0:30 to 4:50 for Reddit; CTR increased from 0.93% to 3.25% for Contextual. Strategy later expanded across additional service lines.

Strategic Lesson: Channel selection must align with intent phase — capturing users during research yields stronger engagement quality.

Objective: Capture relocating audiences entering Houston from Texas and national markets and increasing brand preference.

Approach:

  • Intorduced and tested direct platform buys on Zillow & RE/MAX
  • Targeted relocation-phase property search behavior
  • Positioned healthcare messaging within transition lifecycle stage

Result: Avg Time on site increased from 0:40 to 5:35 for both platforms, producing the highest engagement depth across service lines. Also the preference score improved from 1.15% to 3.70%

Strategic Lesson: Lifecycle intent targeting can outperform traditional demographic segmentation.

Problem: High traffic acquisition failed to convert proportionally.

Analysis:

  • Broad targeting diluted intent
  • Landing engagement was weak
  • Messaging misaligned with user expectations

Optimization Strategy:

  • Refined audience segmentation
  • Improved landing experience
  • Repositioned messaging toward qualification clarity

Result: 62% increase in online registrations.

Strategic Lesson: Traffic without intent does not drive business impact.
