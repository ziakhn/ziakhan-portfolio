Signature Case Studies

Houston Methodist – Reddit & Contextual Display Strategy

Context
Traditional paid media limited reach for sensitive service lines such as cancer and bariatrics.

Strategy
Introduced Reddit and contextual display campaigns to align messaging with research-phase audience behavior rather than relying solely on standard search and social channels.

Channel Approach

  • Reddit: Content-driven messaging placed within relevant health-focused communities aligned with discussion-based research behavior.
  • Contextual Display: Ads positioned alongside medically relevant content instead of broad demographic targeting.

Results

  • Time on site increased from 0:30 → 4:50 for Reddit
  • Contextual display CTR increased from 0.93% → 3.25%

The strategy was later expanded across additional service lines.

Strategic Lesson
Channel selection must align with user intent phase — reaching audiences during research produces stronger engagement.

Houston DMA – New Mover Lifecycle Targeting

Context
Traditional social media campaigns struggled to reach highly qualified audiences for Houston service lines despite generating traffic.

Strategy
Introduced lifecycle-based targeting through real estate platforms to reach individuals actively relocating into the Houston DMA.

Channel Approach

  • Zillow & RE/MAX: Targeted individuals researching properties in the $250K+ range, signaling relocation intent and financial readiness.
  • Lifecycle Alignment: Positioned healthcare messaging during the relocation phase when individuals actively research services in their new city.

Results

  • Average time on site increased from 0:40 → 5:35
  • Preference score improved from 1.15% → 3.70%

Producing the highest engagement depth across service lines.

Strategic Lesson
Lifecycle-intent targeting can outperform traditional demographic segmentation.

Mr. Texas Real Estate – Education Program Optimization

Context
A real estate investment education program generated strong traffic but struggled to convert visitors into paid mastermind memberships.

Strategy
Shifted from volume-based acquisition to intent-driven targeting of high-net-worth individuals interested in investment education and portfolio growth.

Channel Approach

  • LinkedIn Targeting: Reached professionals and investors interested in real estate investment strategies and wealth-building opportunities.
  • Education-Based Messaging: Positioned campaigns around financial education and long-term portfolio growth rather than direct promotion.

Results

  • Mastermind program subscriptions increased by 62%

Significantly improving conversion performance for the high-ticket membership funnel.

Strategic Lesson
Education-driven engagement combined with precise targeting improves conversion for high-value programs.
